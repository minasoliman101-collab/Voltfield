How a subscription streamlines buying & repairs

From breakdown to PO, faster

Generate the component BOM for the failed asset, tick the parts that need replacing, and the shared quote list routes to whoever holds the budget — no spreadsheet ping-pong.

Repair kits, not part hunts

Saved BOMs become reorderable repair kits: seals, gaskets, bolting and wear parts for each asset class, one click to re-quote at current pricing and lead times.

Lead-time risk, surfaced early

Watch the long-lead items that can idle a project — transformers, switchgear, PCS — and get alerted when quoted lead times or indicative pricing move.

Procurement systems, connected

Enterprise punchout and API access put the catalog inside your ERP, so requisitions carry real part numbers, prices, and lead times from the start.
